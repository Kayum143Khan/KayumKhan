About Me

Hi! I’m Kayum Khan — a computer science student, passionate educator, and full-stack web developer based in Dhaka, Bangladesh. Welcome to my personal website, a space where I share my journey through code, creativity, and community-building.

With a strong foundation in technologies like React, Next.js, Tailwind CSS, and Visual Studio, I specialize in building responsive websites and crafting tutorials that make learning web development accessible for everyone. Whether it's a sleek landing page or a structured HTML guide, I’m always thinking about the user experience and how to make information intuitive and engaging.

What drives me is the belief that education should be both inspiring and inclusive. I often describe myself as “a passionate computer science teacher and lifelong learner.” That mindset has fueled everything from building educational apps to creating video tutorials, slideshow-based lessons, and practical content tailored to students' real-world needs.

Recently, I’ve been working on expanding the digital presence of organizations like MK International Student Consultants, designing educational content that is both culturally relevant and visually impactful. I also enjoy mentoring fellow students and helping others navigate their way through the tech world with confidence.

If you’re interested in collaboration, learning something new, or just want to connect — feel free to explore, reach out, or dive into my tutorials. Let’s learn and grow together.
